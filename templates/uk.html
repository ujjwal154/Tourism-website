Tiffin Top Nainital

Tiffin Top, also lovingly called Dorothy’s Seat, is one of the best viewpoints in this beautiful hill station of Uttarakhand. Located at an altitude of 2,292 meters (7,520 feet), it opens up to stunning panoramic scenes of the Kumaon hills and the majestic Himalayas in the distance.

Naina Peak

Earlier called China Peak, this place was renamed to Naina Peak after the 1962 Indo-China war. This change was done to reflect the emotions and sentiments of the locals.

River rafting

Rishikesh rafting is a water-based activity that involves navigating the rapids of the Ganges River on an inflatable raft. The thrill of rafting through the rapids while surrounded by the stunning Himalayan scenery is an experience that is not easily forgotten.

Read more

Nelong Valley

Located in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand, Nelong Valley is a hidden treasure of India. Sitting at an altitude of around 11,000 feet, this mesmerizing valley offers a stunning blend of Himalayan landscapes and a rich history as part of the ancient Indo-Tibetan trade route.

Read more

Vasudhara Fall

Vasudhara Falls is a magnificent waterfall in the Garhwal Himalayas, near Badrinath, known for its spiritual significance and mesmerizing beauty. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, this hidden gem attracts pilgrims and adventure seekers alike. The trek to Vasudhara Falls offers breathtaking views and a deep connection to ancient legends.
